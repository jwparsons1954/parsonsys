Hives and Swarms

A hive on the other hand is a semi permanent home for the bees. A hive is where they have built comb to raise their brood and store honey. The bees in a hive are not nearly so gentle as the ones in a swarm. They are not aggressive, but they are intent upon protecting their home from any and all threats, including YOU !! Opening a hive and disturbing the bees can be EXTREMELY DANGEROUS !!

A swarm of bees are a group of bees who have left their home and are in search of a new place to call home. They are extremely gentle when in a swarm.
