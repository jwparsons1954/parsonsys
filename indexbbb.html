Are you as disappointed and disgusted with the way the debates are being managed as I am ?

        So far I have not seen a real debate, only mini interviews and survivor episodes.

        The media and parties (RNC/DNC) control the questions for these staged debates.

The Town Hall Meetings are better, but still only a few people get to ask questions.

The websites of the Candidates answer the questions they think you want the answers to.

        Many questions are still left unanswered.

 

I know there is a better way to do this.

            All Citizens could ask questions and the most asked questions would get answered.

            All Candidates could answer the questions, just like in a REAL debate.

            All Candidates, no matter what party could take part in the debate.

            We could have a running total of each candidate's popularity.
